# SEO_PAGE_MAP.md

## Strategic principle
MISWΛY must not become a generic SEO website.
It must become a premium, legible, commercially credible artist site.

Each page must have:
- a primary intent,
- a semantic role,
- a conversion role,
- a tone boundary.

---

## 1. Homepage `/`

### Primary role
Brand gate / entry portal

### SEO role
Capture brand understanding at root level without destroying the splashscreen identity.

### Primary intent
- MISWAY
- MISWΛY
- brand-led discovery
- first encounter

### Conversion role
Push users deeper into:
- Explore
- Tracks
- About

### Tone
Minimal, atmospheric, restrained

### Rules
- keep premium splashscreen feel
- no landing-page clutter
- semantic reinforcement allowed only if discreet
- below-the-fold content must remain elegant and understated

---

## 2. About `/about`

### Primary role
Artist credibility page

### SEO role
Help search engines and humans understand:
- who MISWΛY is
- what kind of music/project this is
- what the project stands for

### Primary intent
- MISWAY artist
- MISWAY music project
- about MISWAY
- MISWΛY bio

### Conversion role
Build trust and route users toward:
- tracks
- contact
- SoundCloud
- future artist/EPK page if needed

### Tone
Clear, premium, human, serious, still poetic but less opaque

### Rules
- stronger clarity than homepage
- still not generic
- commercially useful without sounding transactional

---

## 3. Artist or EPK page `/artist` or `/epk`

### Primary role
Most bankable page

### SEO role
Central authority page for artist, music, collaboration, press, sync, and serious discovery.

### Primary intent
- MISWAY music
- MISWAY artist
- electronic music artist MISWAY
- MISWAY collaboration
- MISWAY sync / licensing

### Conversion role
Direct conversion into:
- inquiry
- collaboration
- sync / licensing
- serious listening

### Tone
Clean, premium, credible, commercially literate

### Rules
- highest clarity page
- strongest trust page
- strongest “why this matters” page
- must not feel cheap or over-marketed

---

## 4. Explore `/explore`

### Primary role
Curated entry point

### SEO role
Secondary discovery surface for selected listening

### Primary intent
- explore MISWAY music
- selected tracks / featured music

### Conversion role
Push users into track pages and deeper catalogue exploration

### Tone
Atmospheric but more navigable than homepage

### Rules
- keep elegance
- support discovery
- do not over-explain

---

## 5. Tracks `/tracks`

### Primary role
Catalogue gateway

### SEO role
Capture catalogue-level and discography-style intent

### Primary intent
- MISWAY tracks
- MISWAY songs
- MISWAY catalogue
- MISWAY music archive

### Conversion role
Push users toward:
- specific tracks
- repeat listening
- catalogue depth

### Tone
Clearer, more index-like, still premium

### Rules
- more explicit than homepage
- must feel like a real catalogue page
- should not feel like a database dump

---

## 6. Track detail `/tracks/[slug]`

### Primary role
Work page / music recording page

### SEO role
Capture track-level discovery and provide a serious page for each piece

### Primary intent
- track title + MISWAY
- track title + music
- track title + SoundCloud
- track title + electronic music

### Conversion role
Push users toward:
- listening
- adjacent tracks
- deeper catalogue traversal
- artist discovery

### Tone
Focused, descriptive, elegant, not overblown

### Rules
- each track page must feel legitimate
- include context, not fluff
- structured data must reflect visible content

---

## 7. Drift `/drift`

### Primary role
Alternative discovery surface

### SEO role
Secondary, atmosphere-led semantic surface

### Primary intent
Low-priority direct SEO page; mostly supportive

### Conversion role
Push users toward track listening and exploration

### Tone
Strange, soft, non-linear, but still intelligible

### Rules
- no need to over-optimize
- should remain experience-led
- metadata should still be clean

---

## 8. Contact surfaces

### Primary role
Commercial and human entry point

### SEO role
Low direct SEO priority, high conversion importance

### Conversion role
Enable collaboration, sync, artistic contact, project proposals

### Tone
Simple, serious, open, premium

### Rules
- remove vagueness
- make reasons to contact clearer
- avoid cheap call-to-action patterns

---

## Internal linking logic

Recommended logic:
- Home → Explore / Tracks / About
- About → Tracks / SoundCloud / Contact
- Artist/EPK → About / Tracks / Contact / SoundCloud
- Tracks → Track pages
- Track pages → adjacent tracks / About / SoundCloud
- Drift → Track pages / Tracks

No spammy or excessive cross-linking.
